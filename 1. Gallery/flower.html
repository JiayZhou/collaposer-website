Flower Women

Paper Demo Video
Input Description
A woman enjoys gardening, surrounded by flowers and butterflies, with mountains and trees in the background.
Collaposer Output: Static Collage with Layered Assets (Manually Layout)
Advanced Editing
  • Layer Sequence Adjustment: The butterfly above the woman's head was moved to the back, allowing it to appear from behind her. The blocked mountains were brought foward.
  • Layout Adjustment: We refined the size and position of the women and flowers to better fit the frame. Some flowers were copied and carefully arranged to cover the angular edges of the women's portraits.
  • Visual Effect Adjustment: To create a vintage style and achieve a more cohesive background, we added textures and adjusted the colors of the background cluster.
  • Animation Design and Production: We animated the plant and insect clusters. Butterflies flutter, bees buzz, and flowers sway or grow, while other elements remain static.
