Input Description
A vintage botanical collage with hand-drawn flowers, leaves, and roots on aged paper. It includes faded Latin names, antique labels, and soft earthy tones. Inspired by 19th-century plant journals, it evokes a nostalgic, natural feel.
Collaposer Output: Static Collage with Layered Assets (Manually Layout)
Advanced Editing
  • Layer Sequence Adjustment: We added text between the butterfly cluster and other elements, moving the birds and sea lion in front of the text.
  • Layout Adjustment: We adjusted the position and scale of each layer to highlight the text information.
  • Visual Effect Adjustment: We applied visual adjustments by modifying the colors of clusters and adding various paper textures—especially to the backgrounds—to unify tones and enhance contrast between the characters and their surroundings.
  • Animation Design and Production: In this animated collage, butterflies serve as a visual transition. They flap outward from the center, revealing the poster underneath. In the final scene, two butterflies flap while a sea lion moves from the "B" to the "n" in "Busan" (stop-motion).
