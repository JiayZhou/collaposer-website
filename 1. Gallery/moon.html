The Little Athletes
An Example Made by the Authors
Paper Demo Video
Description
In the space, athletes on planets are playing with moons as balls, while butterflies fly around.
Collaposer Output: Static Collage with Layered Assets (Manually Layout)
Advanced Editing
  • Layout Adjustment: We added and cropped a space layer, tiling it as a starry background. We refined the size and position of the athletes, moons, and flowers to highlight the characters' actions.
  • Visual Effect Adjustment: We created a dazzling starry background using textures and colors, and added some twinkling star effects.
  • Animation Design: In this animated collage, we focus on character animation. On each planet, an athlete either swings at the moon like a badminton shuttle or bounces it like a ball. Each hit makes the moon orbit the planet, while butterflies float motionlessly and mysterious stars flicker in the background.
  • Animation Production: The athletes’ torsos and legs have been separated to support crouching through deformation.
