Giant Cat
An Example Made by P11
Paper Demo Video
Input Description
A big cat is resting in a landscape under a blue sky with some clouds, featuring a huge moon. A man in white and red clothing is jumping on its back, while a woman is lying there as well. In the foreground, there are also some trees and plants.
Collaposer Output: Static Collage with Layered Assets (Manually Layout)
Advanced Editing
  • Layout Adjustment: We cropped the sky layer and copied it to tile as a paper background.
  • Visual Effect Adjustment: We added paper textures to the sky and countryside layers and adjusted the background colors to highlight the foreground, making the entire piece resemble a physical collage.
  • Animation Design: We applied stop-motion effects to all non-character elements. Each layer enters the frame from outside using stop-motion. Once in position, the red man jumps on the cat, causing its back to sag and the blue man to fall off.
  • Animation Production: To achieve the deformation during the red man's jump, we added puppet pins at his joints.
