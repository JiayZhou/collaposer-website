Cats Kick Moon
An Example Made by P3
Paper Demo Video
Input Description
An athlete with a cat head plays volleyball using the moon on the beach.
Collaposer Output: Static Collage with Layered Assets (Manually Layout)
Advanced Editing
  • Layer Sequence Adjustment: The cat playing the guitar and the athlete's hat were brought to the front.
  • Layout Adjustment: We refine the size and position of the athletes and the moon to ensure they do not intersect during movement.
  • Visual Effect Adjustment: To create a mysterious sci-fi atmosphere, we added textures to the beach and sky, and adjusted colors .
  • Animation Design: In this animated collage, we focus on articulated character animation. A man on the right kicks the moon like a ball, demonstrating leg rotation. The moon deforms upon impact and flies toward the man on the left. Simultaneously, the right man rotates 360 degrees due to the force. The moon hits the left man's face, causing both to deform, and then the moon returns to the right.
  • Animation Production: To support the kicking motion, the left athlete’s arm has been segmented, with the shoulder as the rotation center. The right athlete’s arms, upper and lower legs, and feet have been fine-tuned, with each part segmented and assigned to the appropriate joints (shoulder, hip, knee, ankle). The movements of the two athletes are realized through rotation and translation.
